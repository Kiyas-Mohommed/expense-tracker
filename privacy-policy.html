Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Expense Tracker collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Expense Tracker, you may provide us with:

  • Financial Data: Transaction details including amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates that you manually enter
  • Preferences: Your chosen settings such as currency, language, theme, and budget limits
  • Recurring Transactions: Information about recurring income or expenses you configure
1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the application, features used, and time spent
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device (see our Cookie Policy)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Enable expense tracking, budgeting, and financial reporting features
  • Personalization: Customize your experience based on your preferences
  • Data Storage: Save your financial data locally in your browser's storage
  • Improvements: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our application
  • Communication: Send notifications about budget alerts and transaction summaries

Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Expense Tracker primarily stores your data locally on your device using browser localStorage. This means:

  • Your financial data stays on your device
  • Data is not transmitted to external servers by default
  • Clearing browser data will remove your stored information
3.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Data encryption during any transmission
  • Secure coding practices
  • Regular security assessments
Important: We recommend regularly exporting your data as a backup. Data stored in localStorage can be lost if you clear your browser data or use private/incognito mode.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your data
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your stored data within the application
  • Export: Download your data in JSON or CSV format at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual transactions or clear all data
  • Modify: Edit any transaction or preference settings
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and certain features

Cookies and Tracking

Expense Tracker uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our application
  • Improve user experience

For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

Children's Privacy

Expense Tracker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Providing a notice within the application for significant changes

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Website: www.expensetracker.com/contact
