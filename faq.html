Getting Started

Expense Tracker is a free, easy-to-use personal finance management application that helps you:

  • Track your daily income and expenses
  • Categorize transactions for better organization
  • Set and monitor budgets
  • View visual reports and charts
  • Export your data for backup or analysis

All your data is stored locally on your device, ensuring your privacy.

No account required! Expense Tracker works entirely in your browser without requiring registration or login.

Your data is stored locally using your browser's localStorage, which means you can start tracking expenses immediately without any setup.

Yes, completely free! Expense Tracker is 100% free to use with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features locked behind a paywall.

All features are available to everyone.

Getting started is easy:

  1. Add your first transaction: Click the "Add Transaction" button
  2. Choose the type: Select Income or Expense
  3. Fill in details: Enter amount, category, description, and date
  4. Save: Click "Save Transaction"

That's it! Your transaction will appear in the Recent Transactions list and be reflected in your dashboard summary.

Transactions

To add a new transaction:

  1. Click the Add Transaction button
  2. Select whether it's an Income or Expense
  3. Enter the Amount
  4. Choose a Category (e.g., Food, Transport, Salary)
  5. Add a Description for reference
  6. Select the Date
  7. Click Save Transaction

Yes! Each transaction in the Recent Transactions list has action buttons:

  • Edit: Modify any transaction details
  • Duplicate: Create a copy with today's date
  • Delete: Remove the transaction permanently

Income Categories:

💰 Salary, 💼 Business/Freelance, 📈 Investment, 🏘️ Rental Income, 🎉 Bonus, 🎀 Gift Received, ↩️ Refund

📦 Other: For anything that doesn't fit the above categories

Recurring transactions automatically create new entries based on your schedule:

  • Daily: Creates a transaction every day
  • Weekly: Creates a transaction every week
  • Monthly: Creates a transaction every month
  • Yearly: Creates a transaction every year

To set up:

  1. Go to Settings → Recurring Transactions
  2. Click "Add Recurring"
  3. Fill in the details and frequency
  4. Set start date (and optional end date)

You can enable/disable, edit, or delete recurring transactions at any time.

Use the filter options above the transactions list:

  • Type Filter: All, Income Only, or Expenses Only
  • Category Filter: Filter by specific category
  • Period Filter: Today, This Week, This Month, This Year, All Time, or Custom Range
  • Search Box: Search by description or amount

Budgets

To set your monthly budget:

  1. Click the Settings icon in the navigation
  2. Find the Monthly Budget field
  3. Enter your desired budget amount
  4. Click Save Settings

Your budget progress will be displayed on the dashboard with a visual progress bar.

Yes! You can set individual budgets for each expense category:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Scroll to Category Budgets section
  3. Enter budget amounts for each category you want to track
  4. Save your settings

You'll receive alerts when you reach 80%, 90%, or exceed your category budgets.

Budget alerts notify you about your spending:

  • ℹ️ 80% Used - You've used 80% of your budget
  • ⚠️ 90% Used - Warning: approaching your limit
  • ⚠️ Exceeded - You've gone over your budget

You can enable or disable budget alerts in Settings → Notifications.

Data & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage.

Benefits:
  • Your financial data never leaves your device
  • No server or cloud storage involved
  • Complete privacy and control over your data

Regular backups are recommended! To export your data:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Scroll to Data Management
  3. Click Export Data (JSON) for a complete backup
  4. Or click Export as CSV for spreadsheet compatibility
Important: Clearing your browser data or using private/incognito mode will remove your stored data. Always keep backups!

To restore data from a JSON backup:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Click Import Data
  3. Select your backup JSON file
  4. Your data will be restored automatically

Note: Importing will replace all existing data with the backup data.

Since data is stored locally, it doesn't automatically sync between devices. However, you can manually transfer data:

  1. Export your data from the first device (JSON format)
  2. Transfer the file to your other device (email, cloud storage, USB, etc.)
  3. Import the file on the second device

Repeat this process whenever you want to sync your data between devices.

Yes! Your data security is ensured by:

  • Local Storage: Data never leaves your device
  • No External Transmission: We don't send your data to any servers
  • No Third-Party Access: Your financial information isn't shared with anyone
  • Browser Security: Protected by your browser's security measures

For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
